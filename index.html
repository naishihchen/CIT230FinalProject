Let Us Introduce Ourselves!

We are the Temple Inn & Suites, a hotel chain that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. Our owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of those patrons. The hotel accommodations and services are available to anyone willing to abide by the terms and policies outlined by the company.

We offer world class service, whatever your temple-going needs. From beautiful rooms overlooking the temple grounds, to wonderful long-term suites for our temple missionaries. From a gorgeous wedding reception hall to a state of the art family research facility. Book a reservation today from our reservations page, and let Temple Inn and Suites be your home away from home.
